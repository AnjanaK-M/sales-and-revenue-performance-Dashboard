Sales And Revenue Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales and revenue performance across products, regions, and time periods to support data-driven business decisions.

Objectives
- Understand overall sales and revenue trends over time.
- Identify top-performing products, categories, and regions.
- Analyze revenue contribution by customer segment or sales channel.
- Track key performance metrics such as total sales, profit, and growth rate.
- Deliver actionable visual insights for sales and business teams.

Power BI Features Used
- Interactive dashboards and slicers
- Card KPIs (Total Sales, Total Revenue, Profit Margin, Growth %, etc.)
- Bar, column, and line charts for trend analysis
- Drill-through and tooltip features
- Filters on Region, Product Category, Time Period, Customer Segment
- Conditional formatting and trend/comparison visuals

 Key Visual Insights
- **Product Performance**: A small group of products drives the majority of sales.
- **Seasonality**: Sales show noticeable peaks during specific months/quarters.
- **Profit Margins**: Higher revenue doesn't always mean higher profit — margin varies by category.
- **Growth Trends**: Year-over-year comparison highlights growth and decline patterns.

How to Use This Project
1. Clone or download the repository.
2. Open `Sales And Revenue Performance.pbix` using **Power BI Desktop**.
3. Ensure the underlying dataset is placed in the same folder or relink the data source if needed.
4. Interact with the dashboard to explore insights.
